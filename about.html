bikesphere a connected bike community

Connect everyday Brussels cyclists and those who always wanted to start biking
bring us together, create a network and provide the ground for mutual support and exchange
bikesphere is a community (web/app) and physically connects us through a smart device
allowing us to remain connected even when riding our bicycles, giving us recognition and access to a range of features/functionalities
Bikesphere is open source and uses DIY approach to encourage our active participation

By Brussels cyclists for Brussels

Project layout

The App

- it would be possible to interact directly with any members that you cross on your way
- you can reach the community or relevant services in emergency situations, for you and your bike
- you will be kept informed with pertinent newsfeeds, like events, legal updates, infrastructure modifications

Maps

- bike infrastructures
- members in the area & available at this point of time
- bike guide (plan your trip from A to B, taking into account wisdom of the crowd) e.g. avoid problematic zone/crossings
- reviews/feedback of bikeshops/repair shops
- partnership programs with selected partners/retailers

IoT

- who’s around? Give visibility to bikers and facilitate interaction
- alarm function for your bike (smartphone is the key)
- light me up
- proximity warning to other members = WINK
- I need help, who’s around?
